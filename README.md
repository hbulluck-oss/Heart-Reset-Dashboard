## ðŸ“Š Scoring Methodology

### Calibrated Risk Scoring
- **Dynamic Denominator**: Maximum possible points adapt based on individual circumstances
- **Lipid Contribution Cap**: Total lipid scoring capped at 60 points (prevents single factor dominance)
- **Lifestyle Soft Cap**: Lifestyle factors capped at 60 points collectively
- **Weighted Factors**: Clinical significance determines point allocation

### Non-Modifiable vs Modifiable Factors
The assessment clearly distinguishes between:
- **Non-Modifiable**: Age, ethnicity, family history (awareness factors)
- **Modifiable**: Blood pressure, cholesterol, lifestyle choices (actionable factors)

### Traffic Light Thresholds
- **Green (<20%)**: Low cardiovascular load - optimization focus
- **Amber (20-40%)**: Accumulating risk - intervention beneficial
- **Red (â‰¥40%)**: High cardiovascular load - medical evaluation recommended
- **Grey**: Indeterminate due to missing critical parameters
